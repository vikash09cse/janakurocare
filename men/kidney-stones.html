Patient-friendly guide

We provide comprehensive care for common urological conditions affecting men.

What is this?

Kidney stones are hard deposits that form inside your kidneys and can cause unbearable pain while passing through the urinary tract. They are one of the most common urological problems we see, especially in Bihar’s hot and humid climate. The good news — with today’s technology, they’re completely treatable, often without any cuts.

Common symptoms

Talk to us if you notice any of the following.

  • check_circle Sudden, sharp pain in the back or lower abdomen
  • check_circle Burning or pain while urinating
  • check_circle Blood in urine (red or pinkish colour)
  • check_circle Nausea, vomiting, or feeling restless
  • check_circle Frequent urge to urinate but little output
Our treatment

Advanced laser & RIRS — faster recovery, minimal pain

Laser & endoscopic stone care at Janak Uro Care, Siwan, Bihar

At Janak Uro Care, we use advanced laser technology and RIRS (Retrograde Intrarenal Surgery) — a completely no-cut procedure — to break and remove stones safely. Most patients go home the same or next day, and recovery is much faster compared to traditional surgery.

  • check_circle Advanced laser treatment
  • check_circle RIRS (no-cut surgery)
  • check_circle Faster recovery
  • check_circle Minimal pain

When to worry: If you have severe pain with fever, chills, or are unable to urinate — don’t wait. This needs urgent attention.
