Our Laser Procedures

Minimally invasive, precision laser treatments for kidney stones and prostate — with less pain and faster recovery.

medical_services

RIRS – Laser Stone Surgery

Retrograde Intrarenal Surgery uses a flexible ureteroscope and holmium laser to break kidney stones into dust. No incision needed — the scope enters through the natural urinary passage.

  • check_circle No cuts or scars
  • check_circle Day-care procedure
  • check_circle Suitable for stones up to 20mm
  • check_circle Minimal bleeding
biotech

HoLEP – Laser Prostate Surgery

Holmium Laser Enucleation of the Prostate is the gold standard for treating enlarged prostate (BPH). It removes prostate tissue precisely with minimal bleeding.

  • check_circle Safe for large prostates
  • check_circle Shorter catheter time
  • check_circle Quick recovery
  • check_circle Long-lasting results
healing

Laser Lithotripsy

High-powered holmium laser is used to fragment stones in the ureter and kidney into tiny pieces that pass naturally or are extracted.

  • check_circle Works on all stone types
  • check_circle Precise and safe
  • check_circle Outpatient possible
  • check_circle Minimal discomfort
