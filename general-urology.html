Accurate Diagnosis Before Every Treatment

We use the full range of modern urological investigations so every treatment decision is precise and evidence-based.

1 Flexible Cystoscopy

Camera examination of the bladder to detect cancer, stones, and inflammation.

2 Ultrasound (KUB)

First-line imaging for stones, cysts, tumours, and bladder conditions.

3 Urine Analysis & Culture

Identifies infections, blood cells, cancer markers, and stone risk.

4 Urodynamic Studies

Measures bladder pressure and function for incontinence and voiding disorders.

5 CT Urogram / MRI

Detailed cross-sectional imaging for stone mapping and cancer staging.

6 Prostate Biopsy (TRUS/MRI-Fusion)

Guided tissue sampling to confirm or exclude prostate cancer.
