Male Urological Conditions We Specialise In

From common kidney stones to complex cancers, our team provides advanced, minimally invasive care for all male urological problems.

diamond

Kidney Stones

Kidney stones are hard mineral deposits that cause intense flank pain and urinary blockage. We offer painless laser removal (RIRS/PCNL) with same-day or next-day discharge.

elderly_man

Prostate Enlargement (BPH)

Benign Prostatic Hyperplasia (BPH) causes urinary slowness and frequent nighttime urination in men over 50. We offer HoLEP laser surgery, Urolift, Rezum, and Aquablation for lasting relief.

biotech

Prostate Cancer

Prostate cancer is highly treatable when detected early and our team uses the latest minimally invasive techniques. We offer HIFU, Nanoknife, and laparoscopic surgery for organ-sparing treatment.

water_drop

Bladder Cancer

Bladder cancer often presents as painless blood in urine and requires prompt evaluation. We perform cystoscopy, TURBT, and laparoscopic radical cystectomy for comprehensive management.

hub

Kidney Cancer

Kidney cancer is often discovered incidentally on scans and is very treatable at early stages. We offer laparoscopic partial or radical nephrectomy with faster recovery and minimal scarring.

child_care

Male Infertility

Male infertility is commonly caused by varicocele, hormonal imbalance, or ductal blockages. We perform varicocelectomy, sperm retrieval, and provide a full fertility-focused evaluation.

favorite

Erectile Dysfunction

Erectile dysfunction affects men of all ages and can signal underlying vascular or hormonal issues. We provide confidential, personalised medical or surgical treatment plans.

local_hospital

Urinary Tract Infection (UTI)

UTIs in men are less common but require thorough investigation to rule out structural causes. We diagnose and treat recurrent UTIs with targeted therapy and full urological assessment.

opacity

Hematuria (Blood in Urine)

Blood in urine is a warning sign that should never be ignored, even when painless. We perform urine cytology, ultrasound, and cystoscopy to find and treat the underlying cause.

pediatrics

Pediatric Urology Problems

Children can suffer from undescended testes, hypospadias, hydronephrosis, and recurrent UTIs. Our team provides gentle, child-friendly surgical and non-surgical urological care.
