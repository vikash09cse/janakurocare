Frequently Asked Questions

For any queries regarding the procedure, go through this FAQ section.

What are common urological problems?expand_more

Common urological problems include kidney stones, urinary tract infections (UTIs), benign prostatic hyperplasia (BPH), prostate issues, bladder conditions, and conditions requiring minimally invasive or laser surgery. Our team at JanakUROCare diagnoses and treats a wide range of urological conditions.

When should I consult a urologist?expand_more

You should consult a urologist if you experience persistent urinary symptoms (pain, frequency, urgency, blood in urine), kidney or flank pain, difficulty urinating, erectile or fertility concerns, or if your doctor recommends a specialist. Early consultation can help in accurate diagnosis and timely treatment.

Is laser surgery painful?expand_more

Laser surgery at JanakUROCare is typically minimally invasive and performed under appropriate anaesthesia. Most patients experience minimal pain during the procedure and a relatively quick recovery with less discomfort compared to traditional open surgery. Pain management is provided as needed.

What is the recovery time after laser stone surgery?expand_more

Recovery time depends on the type of procedure. Many patients undergoing RIRS or similar laser stone surgery are discharged within a day or two and can resume light activities soon after. Your doctor will give you specific instructions based on your procedure and overall health.

Does Janak UroCare accept insurance?expand_more

JanakUROCare works with various insurance and payment options. Please contact our clinic with your insurance details to confirm coverage for your consultation and procedures. Our staff will assist you with billing and claim-related queries.
