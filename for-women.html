Female Urological Conditions We Specialise In

Our experienced team treats the full spectrum of female urological conditions with sensitivity, expertise, and the latest minimally invasive technology.

Urinary Tract Infection (UTI)

UTIs are one of the most common infections in women, caused by bacteria entering the urinary tract. We provide targeted antibiotic treatment and investigate recurrent cases for underlying structural causes.

Urinary Incontinence

Urinary incontinence — leaking urine on coughing, sneezing, or urgency — affects millions of women and is highly treatable. We offer pelvic floor therapy, bladder retraining, and surgical options like TVT/TOT sling procedures.

Read more arrow_forward
diamond

Kidney Stones

Kidney stones cause severe flank pain and can affect women at any age, especially during pregnancy. We offer painless laser stone removal (RIRS/PCNL) with minimal recovery time.

Read more arrow_forward
opacity

Hematuria (Blood in Urine)

Blood in urine is a warning sign that should always be investigated promptly, even if painless. We perform urine cytology, ultrasound, and cystoscopy to identify and treat the underlying cause.

Read more arrow_forward
female

Overactive Bladder (OAB)

Overactive bladder causes sudden, uncontrollable urges to urinate and frequent trips to the bathroom. We offer bladder retraining, medications, Botox bladder injections, and Interstim neuromodulation therapy.

Read more arrow_forward
hub

Bladder Prolapse (Cystocele)

Bladder prolapse occurs when the bladder drops into the vaginal wall, causing pressure and urinary symptoms. We offer surgical repair options including minimally invasive laparoscopic sacrocolpopexy.

Read more arrow_forward
monitor_heart

Interstitial Cystitis

Interstitial cystitis is a chronic bladder condition causing persistent pelvic pain and urinary urgency. We provide a thorough diagnosis and a personalised management plan including bladder instillations and pain therapy.

Read more arrow_forward
vaccines

Urethral Stricture

Urethral narrowing in women can cause difficulty urinating, recurrent UTIs, and urinary retention. We offer urethral dilation and surgical reconstruction to restore normal urinary flow.

Urological Issues in Pregnancy

Pregnancy can trigger UTIs, kidney stones, and urinary incontinence due to anatomical and hormonal changes. Our team provides safe, pregnancy-friendly urological care in close coordination with your obstetrician.

Vaginal Rejuvenation – MonaLisa Touch

MonaLisa Touch laser therapy restores vaginal tissue structure by stimulating natural collagen production. It effectively treats dryness, discomfort, and urinary symptoms caused by menopause or childbirth.
